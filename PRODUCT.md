# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The primary users are parked Tesla listeners and passengers following music during charging, waiting, or road trips. They view the app on a large landscape touchscreen in a dim cabin and should not need to type or repeatedly touch the display after signing in. A secondary audience uses the same companion experience on desktop or mobile browsers.

## Product Purpose

Tesla Lyrics is a personal Spotify companion display that shows calm, synchronized lyrics for the song playing on the user's active Spotify device. It does not play audio. Success means the correct lyrics appear quickly, stay visibly in sync, recover quietly from network and playback changes, and feel like a native part of the parked-car experience.

## Brand Personality

Calm, cinematic, dependable. The experience should feel focused and premium without becoming theatrical, gamified, or visually noisy.

## Anti-references

- Karaoke arcade interfaces with bouncing words, scoring, confetti, or constant animation.
- Generic Spotify dashboards crowded with recommendations, history, analytics, and secondary navigation.
- Bright automotive screens, dense settings, tiny controls, and keyboard-first workflows.
- Decorative glassmorphism, neon gradients, and motion that competes with the lyrics.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the lyric as the single focal point.
2. Prefer automatic recovery and sensible defaults over required interaction.
3. Make every optional control large, temporary, and safe for a parked automotive touchscreen.
4. Preserve the last useful state through playback, network, and provider failures.
5. Add independent value to the listening experience without turning the app into a replacement Spotify client.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain WCAG AA contrast or better, large touch targets, color-independent active states, configurable lyric size, reduced-motion support, readable non-Latin and right-to-left scripts, and layouts usable from varied seating distances. Avoid pure-white flashes and motion that can cause discomfort in a dim cabin.
